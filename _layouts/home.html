Hello world!

And welcome to my little website. I'm Juan, a game development, programming, and computer graphics enthusiast who recently graduated from the BYU CS animation and games program.

I love participating in the creation of video games, from any stage of development and from any discipline. I've worked on 3D art, specifically modeling and texturing environments, I've endured more technical tasks, such as ray tracing programming and video rendering, and finally, I've specialized in game design and gameplay programming.
Most recently, I worked on Skyguard, BYU's 2025 capstone project. My participation in the project includes pre-production prototyping, level design, systems programming, and gameplay programming, where I worked as the NPC AI lead.
